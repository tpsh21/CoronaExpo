Welcome!

This is a simple game to visualize exponential growth. Your job is quite easy:

  • Click on the hospital to treat a sick emoji
  • The number of sick emojis doubles after every two (simulated) days
  • The time that you have for each iteration is limited

Disclaimer: This game is neither meant to be scientifically accurate nor to simulate a real Covid19 infection rate. It's sole purpose is to demonstrate the impact of exponential growth.
